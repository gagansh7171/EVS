Recycling

The aphorism is so tired it almost might seem like "reduce, reuse, recycle" should go without saying. Reducing the amount that we consume, and shifting our consumption to well-designed products and services, is the first step. Finding constructive uses for "waste" materials is next. If it's broken, fix it don't replace it! If you can, return it to the producer. Tossing it in the blue bin should be last.

Eco-Friendly Techniques

Eat less meat and egg.
Use Paper Less And Recycle More.
Start A Compost Pile Or Bin.
Cut Down on Energy in your home. (will also lead to smaller energy bills. Its a win win for you and Earth).

Waste Reduction

Ditch the plastic bags.
Make a meal plan- take only as much you can eat.
Start relying more on reusable containers and switch to those hotels offering edible crockery.
Stop using disposable plates and plastic bottles.
